How to eat Oreos

  1. open up the Oreo box/carton
  2. tear open the plastik bag
  3. take out on Oreo cookie

    4. How to Make a Double oreo.

    1. take two Oreos out of the box
    2. peel the top of one oreo of
    3. place the second oreo on top
    4. put back the top part
    5. Enjoy!
